
The List of Commands
########## PAGE 1 - NORMAL ###########
1. source - Direct link to Github
2. quit - exit - Terminates the task.
3. echo - Prints out your Message
4. screenCreate - Create screens. (semi-bugged, unfinished)
5. clearscreen - Clears the screen.
6. shellVer - displays the version/build of the shell.
7. Math (MathAdd, MathSubt) - Calculator (unfinished)
8. Fun (helloMe, 8Ball, YesOrNo) - Some games.
